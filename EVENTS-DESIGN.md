# Todo of the Loom — Enterprise Events List

**Tone rules.** Each event is a single evocative sentence stated as objective fact. No editorial adjectives ("shady," "brilliant," "tragic"), no gates, no magnitudes, no upgrade interactions. Imagery and specificity do the work, not modifiers. Companion building hooks noted at the end.

**Timeline rule.** The game begins in the **present day** and only goes forward. No medieval kings, no Victorian queens, no Chartist rallies, no royal patents, no peerages, no Pre-Raphaelites. Early tiers are modern solo makers, modern brands, and modern factories — the industrial and aristocratic language is reclaimed as *corporate* language (dynasty, magnate, house, collection, honor).

**Tier scaffold** (from `app.js` level-title ladder):

- **Tier 1 — The Craft.** Modern solo maker, home studio, Etsy / craft-fair era (lv. 0–30). *A hand-craft revivalist circle treats automation as sacrilege.*
- **Tier 2 — The Trade.** Modern small brand scaling through wholesale, trade shows, tariffs, customs (lv. 30–57). *A new Luddite movement targets trade-show floors.*
- **Tier 3 — The Mill.** Contract manufacturing, robotics on the floor, OSHA, organized labor (lv. 57–78). *Luddite revival cells live here.*
- **Tier 4 — The Aristocracy.** Corporate dynasty, fashion-week hegemony, philanthropy, politics (lv. 78–114). *A rival-family heir's manifesto lives here.*
- **Tier 5 — The Corporate Oligarchy.** Trusts, antitrust, company towns (lv. 114–155). *Unionization lives here.*
- **Tier 6 — The Technocratic Playboy.** Near-future silicon, AI, espionage (lv. 155–218). *AI-2027 arc and Neo-Luddites live here.*
- **Tier 7 — The Posthuman Upload.** Uploaded consciousness, legal personhood (lv. 218–270). *Anti-upload militants live here.*
- **Tier 8 — The Cosmic Megastructure.** Orbital and planet-scale weaving (lv. 270–360). *First Contact and interplanetary temple-bombers live here.*
- **Tier 9 — The Galactic Hegemon.** Nebulae, starforges, intergalactic (lv. 360–475). *Spiral-arm Luddites live here.*
- **Tier 10 — Universal Transcendence.** Multiversal, omnifabric, heat-death (lv. 475+). *The final-loom sabotage lives here.*

Events are listed under the tier at which they most naturally fire. Late-game tiers inherit from earlier ones — a Luddite-analog fires at **every human-Earth tier** and every post-human tier in a new costume.
